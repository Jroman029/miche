An online portfolio (may also be called a digital portfolio or e-portfolio) is an online representation of work you have created,
as well as your skills and experiences.
It could be a website, blog, or even a video channel.
It could include your resume, a summary of qualifications and skills, images of class projects or design samples, audio and video, charts and schematics, process flows, and more.
